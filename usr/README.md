# SimpleText Notes App

A clean, modern cross-platform note-taking app that uses plain text files (.txt) stored locally on your device.

## Features
- **Local Text Files**: Choose a folder on your device to store all your notes as standard `.txt` files.
- **Clean Interface**: Minimal and modern design focused on your content.
- **Full-Text Search**: Instantly find phrases across all your documents.
- **Tags & Favorites**: Organize notes with custom tags and pin your most important notes to the top.
- **Auto-save & Undo/Redo**: Never lose work with automatic saving and history controls.
- **Version History**: Keep track of changes and easily restore previous versions of any note.
- **Word Count & Timestamps**: View document statistics at a glance.

## Getting Started

1. Run the app: `flutter run`
2. Open Settings (the gear icon on the Home Screen).
3. Select a folder on your device where you want to store your text files.
4. Start creating and editing notes!
